DM Notes

BloodRaven is the party's primary damage tank with 54 HP and two magic items. The Sunken Fang Spear's 1 Charge ability should be tracked carefully — it likely recharges on a long rest (confirm with item entry). Improved Critical (19-20) + Savage Attacker (reroll damage dice, take higher) creates high variance burst damage. Remind the player that Second Wind's Tactical Shift lets them move after activation without OAs — strong for repositioning. Action Surge can be devastating combined with the spear's thrown charge.
