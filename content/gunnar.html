DM Notes

Hunter's Lore is highly useful at FAIL Academy — most creatures encountered have unusual immunities worth flagging. Colossus Slayer rewards targeting already-wounded enemies; coordinate with BloodRaven's burst damage. The Improviser's Satchel and lock pick suggest a roguish side. The Three-Dragon Ante set may be a social hook.
