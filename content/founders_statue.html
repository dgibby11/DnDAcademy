A prominent statue at the heart of campus, visible from most directions. The plaque does not name which founder it depicts.

DM Only

Known use: ritual gathering site for the [[secret_society|Secret Society]] ([[session_2]]).

The statue depicts one of the three founding figures; founding records do not specify which. The choice of which founder to memorialize in the most visible location on campus — and why the others were not similarly honored — has never been officially explained.

The statue serves as an anchor point for deeper magical systems connected to the academy's founding. Its significance to the [[secret_society|Secret Society]]'s [[ninth_thesis|Ninth Thesis]] rituals suggests it is more than decorative.
