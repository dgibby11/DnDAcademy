Location
Northeast area, elevated / mountainside terrain
Focus
Arcane magic, transmutation, magical theory

Housed in a distinctive mountainside facility in the northeast. The elevated position provides isolation for dangerous magical experiments and protects the rest of campus from containment failures.

[[oswald_crumb|Crumb]]'s tower (Arcane Studies) is associated with this area. Access to the tower is controlled; a letter of introduction from a senior faculty member (e.g., Dr. Voss) is required for non-students to enter.
