Location
East of the old library

A small garden area adjacent to the old library, containing memorials to the academy's founders. Most students pass through it without stopping. It is not prominently marked on campus maps.

DM Only

The garden contains the tomb of [[aldric_voss|Aldric Voss]], one of the academy's three original founders. The tomb is modest — a stone marker, no effigies, no inscription beyond name and dates. The tomb is empty. This is not publicly known.

RESTRICTED: Aldric Voss's empty tomb is a critical discovery point for players investigating the founding. Do not surface this detail until players actively investigate the garden.
