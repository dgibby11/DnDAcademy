Backstory

Caelum hails from a small village far from the bustling cities. Driven by wanderlust, he arrived in Neverwinter two years ago and quickly built a reputation performing on street corners and in taverns. He has since taken on occasional work for Lord Neverember — gathering information or swaying public opinion subtly. His life motto: "A song can soothe even the most savage beast, but always have an exit plan."

DM Notes

Caelum's DC 15 save and +7 attack are the highest in the party — Hold Person and Sleep can trivialize single-target fights. Cutting Words is easy to forget as a reaction; remind the player. The Lord Neverember connection is from a previous campaign and may carry over as backstory flavor or hooks. The Actor feat means Caelum can impersonate voices — potential for social infiltration on campus.
