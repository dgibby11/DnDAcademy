Nezznar, the Black Spider

NPC · Villains

A drow male wizard and the principal villain of the adventure. Nezznar has spent years searching for [[wave_echo_cave]] and the [[forge_of_spells|Forge of Spells]] within it. He believes the Forge will let him craft powerful magic items that can elevate his standing among the drow and beyond.

He manipulated the [[cragmaw_tribe]] into serving him, installed [[iarno_albrek]] as his agent in Phandalin, and arranged the capture of [[gundren_rockseeker]] to obtain the map to the cave. When the adventure's final act begins, Nezznar is already inside [[wave_echo_cave]].

Appearance

A lean drow male with pale white hair and violet eyes. He wears dark robes and carries the [[spider_staff]]. Accompanied by drow companions and giant spiders as a matter of preference and practicality.

DM Notes

Nezznar is CR 4 — a serious caster at this level but not overwhelming if the party is prepared. He uses the Drow Mage stat block and supplements it with the [[spider_staff]] (Poison Spray, Spider Climb, Web). He keeps [[nundro_rockseeker]] as a hostage in Area 19.

Strategy: Nezznar will negotiate if cornered. He can offer the location of the Forge of Spells (already known by this point), information about the drow underdark network, or simply his life in exchange for freedom. He's not suicidally loyal to his mission if it means dying.

If captured: He's a valuable prisoner — the Lords' Alliance, Harpers, and others would want to interrogate him about drow activities. He won't give up information easily; Intimidation or long-term leverage is needed. He can be ransomed back to the drow, which has its own consequences.

The real question: Why did a drow come to the surface to chase a human mine? Nezznar is working without full drow sanction — he's an entrepreneur. The Forge of Spells represents a path to power that the drow nobility would not share with him voluntarily. This can be a springboard for future adventures if the party wants to follow up on drow plots.
