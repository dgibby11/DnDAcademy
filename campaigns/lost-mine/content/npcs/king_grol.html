King Grol

NPC · Villains

The bugbear chieftain of the [[cragmaw_tribe|Cragmaw Tribe]], ruling from [[cragmaw_castle]]. Grol is brutal, greedy, and cunning by bugbear standards — which means he can hold a coalition of goblins and hobgoblins together through fear and occasional violence.

He's holding [[gundren_rockseeker]] prisoner on orders from [[nezznar_black_spider|the Black Spider]], who is paying him well for the favor. Grol's loyalty is purely financial.

DM Notes

King Grol fights alongside his dire wolf and a doppelganger ally (working for Nezznar). He has Gundren's map but can't read it — he's been waiting for the Black Spider's emissary to collect it.

Grol can be persuaded or bribed. He'd sell Gundren to the highest bidder if the party makes a compelling offer — he doesn't care about the map itself, only about the payment he was promised. DC 20 Persuasion (lower with a tangible bribe) to negotiate Gundren's release.

The doppelganger is the bigger threat tactically — it can impersonate party members to sow confusion. Have it wait for the right moment to change forms in combat.
