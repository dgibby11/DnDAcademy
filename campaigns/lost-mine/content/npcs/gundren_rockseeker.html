Gundren Rockseeker

NPC · Key NPCs

A dwarf prospector and the party's employer. Gundren hired the party to escort a wagon of supplies to [[barthens_provisions|Barthen's Provisions]] in Phandalin. He rode ahead with [[sildar_hallwinter]] to arrange things — and promptly disappeared.

Gundren is enthusiastic, entrepreneurial, and recklessly optimistic. He's spent years searching the Sword Coast for evidence of the legendary lost mine of Phandelver. He found it — and that discovery cost him his freedom.

Appearance

Stocky dwarf in his middle years, iron-grey beard, calloused hands. Wears practical travelling clothes and carries a battered leather satchel everywhere (normally contains his maps and notes).

DM Notes

Status at campaign start: Captured by [[king_grol]] at [[cragmaw_castle]] on orders from [[nezznar_black_spider|the Black Spider]], who wants Gundren's map to [[wave_echo_cave]]. Gundren is injured but alive. His map was taken.

What he knows: The location of Wave Echo Cave (memorized — he drew the map from memory). That the ancient Forge of Spells is there. That his brothers [[tharden_rockseeker]] and [[nundro_rockseeker]] went ahead to the cave to begin surveying. He doesn't know about the Black Spider's full plan.

Rescue arc: Freeing Gundren is a major goal for both the party and [[sildar_hallwinter]]. Once freed, Gundren wants to go to the cave immediately (even injured). He can lead the party there. If Tharden is dead (which he is), Gundren takes it hard — they were very close.

End of adventure: If Wave Echo Cave is cleared, Gundren offers the party a 10% share of all profits from the mine going forward — a significant long-term reward and an excellent hook for further adventures based out of Phandalin.
