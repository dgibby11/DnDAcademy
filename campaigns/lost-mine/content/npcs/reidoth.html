Reidoth

NPC · Notable Creatures

A weathered human druid and member of the [[emerald_enclave|Emerald Enclave]], living as a wanderer in the wilderness north of Phandalin. [[qelline_alderleaf]] knows him and suggests the party seek him out. He's currently sheltering in the [[ruins_of_thundertree]], watching [[venomfang|the dragon]] that recently moved in.

Reidoth knows more about this land than anyone alive — including the locations of [[cragmaw_castle]] and [[wave_echo_cave]]. He won't share these freely. He'll give the locations in exchange for one favor: drive Venomfang out of Thundertree.

DM Notes

Reidoth is calm, observant, and economical with words. He doesn't dislike the party but has no sentiment about them either — they're useful or they aren't. He's been studying Venomfang and believes the dragon can be driven off without a fight if handled cleverly.

If the party kills Venomfang, Reidoth is satisfied but unsurprised. If they diplomatically convince the dragon to leave, he's quietly impressed. Either way, he delivers: he'll guide the party to [[cragmaw_castle]] or [[wave_echo_cave]], or give them detailed directions if they prefer to go alone.

Emerald Enclave: Reidoth can sponsor party members into the Enclave. The Enclave values those who protect the natural world. His alliance is particularly useful for wilderness travel — Enclave contacts can provide shelter, healing, and local knowledge throughout the North.
