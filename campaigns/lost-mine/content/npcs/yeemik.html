Yeemik

NPC · Villains

A goblin and the second-in-command at [[cragmaw_hideout]], seething with resentment toward his boss [[klarg]]. Yeemik is smarter than most goblins — smart enough to see an opportunity when the party arrives.

He holds [[sildar_hallwinter]] captive as a bargaining chip and will offer to trade Sildar for Klarg's head. Whether the party upholds their end of the deal is their business.

DM Notes

Yeemik is a social encounter in a combat dungeon — use him to teach players that goblins aren't just mooks. He's self-serving but not stupid. He'll give the party useful information (that Gundren was taken somewhere else, that a "Spider" gave the orders) in exchange for help against Klarg.

If Klarg dies and the party spares Yeemik, he takes over the hideout and abandons the Cragmaw mission — he just wants to survive and eat. He's not a long-term threat if left alone. He might even become a crude contact if the party wants information from goblin territory, though he's completely unreliable.
