Tharden Rockseeker

NPC · Key NPCs

The eldest of the three Rockseeker brothers. Tharden and [[nundro_rockseeker|Nundro]] arrived at [[wave_echo_cave]] ahead of [[gundren_rockseeker|Gundren]] to begin surveying. [[nezznar_black_spider|The Black Spider]] arrived first and had Tharden killed. His body lies in Area 4 of Wave Echo Cave.

DM Notes

Tharden is dead when the party arrives. His body — and the magic battleaxe [[hew]] lying near it — tells the story of what happened. He put up a fight. His death hits [[gundren_rockseeker|Gundren]] hard; use this moment for emotional weight if Gundren is with the party when they find the body.

Tharden was a practical, no-nonsense dwarf — the steady one of the three brothers. Nundro was the academic, Gundren the visionary. The loss of Tharden changes the texture of what the mine victory means.
