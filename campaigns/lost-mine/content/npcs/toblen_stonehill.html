Toblen Stonehill

NPC · Phandalin Locals

The proprietor of the [[stonehill_inn|Stonehill Inn]], a stout and friendly human man in his early forties. He moved to Phandalin from Triboar a few years ago to take advantage of the new settlement, and now runs the most important social hub in town.

Toblen is cautious — he has a wife and son to protect, and the Redbrands have already visited the inn once to remind him of that. He's helpful to the party once he's sure they can actually handle trouble.

Rumors Toblen Shares

  • The Redbrands murdered the woodcutter Thel Dendrar and took his family.
  • Someone called Glasstaff runs the Redbrands from under Tresendar Manor.
  • A traveler named Sildar Hallwinter arrived in Phandalin a few days before the party; currently recovering from injuries at the inn.
  • The Rockseeker brothers rented rooms here weeks ago. Nundro and Tharden left for the hills; Gundren never arrived.
