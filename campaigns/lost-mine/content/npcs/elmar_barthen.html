Elmar Barthen

NPC · Phandalin Locals

Owner and operator of [[barthens_provisions]], the largest trading post in Phandalin. A lean, cheerful man in his late forties, always happy to see customers. He's been in Phandalin since the resettlement and has built his business from nothing.

Elmar is the party's first contact in Phandalin — he was expecting [[gundren_rockseeker]] and the supply wagon. When the party arrives without Gundren, he's immediately worried.

DM Notes

Elmar pays the party 10 gp for the delivered supplies (as Gundren arranged). He knows Gundren was excited about a big discovery — "the old mine of Phandelver, back in the hills" — but Gundren was secretive about details. He can direct the party to [[sildar_hallwinter]] if Sildar's name comes up.

Elmar has heard rumors about the Redbrands but stays out of town politics — he just wants to sell goods. He'll give the party a 10% discount on future purchases if they clear out the Redbrand problem (enlightened self-interest).
