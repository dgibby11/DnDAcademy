Nundro Rockseeker

NPC · Key NPCs

The youngest of the three Rockseeker brothers — a scholarly dwarf who handled the engineering and mapping side of the brothers' prospecting operation. He traveled to [[wave_echo_cave]] with [[tharden_rockseeker|Tharden]] to begin surveying while [[gundren_rockseeker|Gundren]] arranged logistics in Phandalin.

[[nezznar_black_spider|The Black Spider]] took him prisoner — alive, because Nundro's knowledge of mining and engineering makes him useful. He's held in Area 19 of Wave Echo Cave when the party arrives.

DM Notes

Nundro is thin, bookish, and visibly frightened — he's been a prisoner for days in a cave full of monsters. He can tell the party everything he knows about Wave Echo Cave's layout (he spent time mapping before his capture) and confirm that [[tharden_rockseeker|Tharden]] is dead (he heard the fighting).

Once rescued, Nundro is eager to help restore the mine. Along with Gundren, he provides the technical expertise to actually operate the [[forge_of_spells|Forge of Spells]] — the party needs both brothers alive to realize the mine's full potential. Nundro is the one who figures out how to restart the Forge.
