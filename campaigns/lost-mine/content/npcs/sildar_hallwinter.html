Sildar Hallwinter

NPC · Key NPCs

A retired soldier and agent of the [[lords_alliance|Lords' Alliance]], hired by [[gundren_rockseeker]] as a travelling companion. Sildar is in his fifties, fit and capable despite his age — a broad-shouldered man with a weathered face and the quiet confidence of someone who has been in difficult situations before.

He was captured alongside Gundren during the goblin ambush and is held prisoner at [[cragmaw_hideout]]. Rescuing him is the first major goal of Part 1.

Goals in Phandalin

  • Find [[iarno_albrek]] — a Lords' Alliance wizard who was sent to Phandalin two months ago to establish order and has not been heard from since.
  • Help rescue [[gundren_rockseeker]] from [[cragmaw_castle]].
  • Restore order in Phandalin. He'll set up in the [[townmasters_hall]] as an unofficial authority once he recovers.

DM Notes

Sildar is an excellent long-term NPC ally. Once rescued and healed, he stays in Phandalin and becomes a reliable contact — he can offer 500 gp for information leading to the location of [[cragmaw_castle]], and another 500 gp for capturing [[iarno_albrek|Glasstaff]] alive.

He is genuinely good-natured and grateful. He won't press the party but is clearly glad to have them. If the party later reveals that Iarno Albrek IS Glasstaff, Sildar is visibly shaken — they were friends once. He'll still want Iarno brought to justice, but he'll be sad about it.

Stats: Use the Veteran stat block (MM p. 350). Armed with longsword and dagger; wears chainmail.
