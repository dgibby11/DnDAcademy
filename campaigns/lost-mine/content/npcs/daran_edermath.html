Daran Edermath

NPC · Phandalin Locals

A retired adventurer of some forty-five years — a half-elf who spent decades ranging across the North before settling in Phandalin to grow apples. Daran runs the [[edermath_orchard|Edermath Orchard]] with an easy competence that makes clear he could still handle himself in a fight if it came to it.

He's a member of the [[order_of_the_gauntlet|Order of the Gauntlet]] and has been disturbed by recent reports of undead activity at [[old_owl_well]]. He asks the party to investigate.

DM Notes

Daran is trustworthy, straightforward, and appreciates competence. He respects the party once they've demonstrated they can fight — he won't explain his Order membership unless asked, but he doesn't hide it either.

Old Owl Well quest: He wants the undead stopped and the wizard responsible confronted. He's not asking for violence — just for the problem to go away. If the party negotiates with [[hamun_kost]] and gets a commitment to leave the dead alone, Daran considers that acceptable.

If party members are interested in the Order of the Gauntlet, Daran sponsors their induction. Order benefit: access to Order patrol networks and safe shelter at Order temples throughout the North.
