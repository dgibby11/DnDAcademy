Sister Garaele

NPC · Phandalin Locals

A young elf cleric of Tymora who tends the [[shrine_of_luck|Shrine of Luck]] in Phandalin. She's quick, enthusiastic, and genuinely faithful — not naive, but optimistic. She's also a covert agent of the [[harpers|Harpers]] and has been tasked with a mission she's had difficulty completing on her own.

Harper Quest

Garaele recently tried to negotiate with [[agatha|Agatha the Banshee]] near [[conyberry]] to find a missing spellbook ([[bowgentles_spellbook]]). Agatha drove her away before she could even speak. She asks the party to try — bring a gift of a silver jeweled comb and approach respectfully. Reward for success: three potions of healing + Harper faction consideration.

What Garaele knows about the Harpers: She can explain the organization and offer to sponsor party members who want to join. Harper benefit: access to Harper safehouses, healing, and information networks. She can also cast spells for the party at no cost if they're members (within reason).
