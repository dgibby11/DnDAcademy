Qelline Alderleaf

NPC · Phandalin Locals

A cheerful halfling farmer in her forties who runs [[alderleaf_farm]] on the southern edge of Phandalin. She knows everyone in town, pays attention, and remembers what she hears. Despite her apparently domestic concerns, she's one of the sharpest sources of local intelligence.

What She Knows

  • She knows the druid [[reidoth]] — he passes through the region occasionally. She believes he would know the locations of [[cragmaw_castle]] and perhaps [[wave_echo_cave]]. He was last seen heading toward the ruins of [[ruins_of_thundertree]].
  • Her nephew Carp has seen people going in and out of a secret tunnel near [[tresendar_manor]] — Redbrands using a hidden entrance. Carp can show the party if they ask.
