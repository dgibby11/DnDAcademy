Hamun Kost

NPC · Notable Creatures

A Red Wizard of Thay — a lean, tattooed human male in red robes — currently excavating [[old_owl_well]] with a retinue of twelve zombies as his labor force and security detail. He's researching the site's Netherese history and is not immediately hostile, though he's not welcoming either.

DM Notes

Kost is morally grey. He's doing something mildly distasteful (undead labor) in a remote location. He'll negotiate if the party opens diplomatically. His request: information about the wraith [[mormesk]] who reportedly died in [[wave_echo_cave]] during the great battle five centuries ago. He's researching arcane history and wants primary source details about Mormesk's death and what spells he was working on.

If the party later returns with details about Mormesk (from actually visiting [[wave_echo_cave]]), Kost pays 150 gp in gems and dismantles his zombie perimeter as a gesture of good faith.

Don't build him up as a recurring villain — he's meant to demonstrate moral complexity and teach players that not every dangerous-looking person is an enemy. Red Wizards are associated with evil in the lore, which makes it interesting when this one just wants to do field research.
