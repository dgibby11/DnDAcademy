Harbin Wester

NPC · Phandalin Locals

The elected Townmaster of Phandalin — a fat, pompous, and deeply cowardly human man in his fifties. He handles administrative matters for the town and tries his hardest to avoid any situation that might require courage or confrontation. He runs the [[townmasters_hall]].

When asked about the Redbrand problem, he dismisses it: "Just rambunctious lads who've had a bit too much to drink." He's been bribed and knows it, though he wouldn't say so out loud.

DM Notes

Harbin is comic relief with a useful function. He's posted the orc bounty at [[wyvern_tor]] because merchants complained and he had to do something. He genuinely wants the party to solve the orc problem — he just doesn't want to be anywhere near the solution.

After the [[redbrands]] are cleared, Harbin becomes suddenly cooperative — almost embarrassingly grateful. He offers the party official recognition as the town's appointed protectors (no salary, but the title matters for certain social interactions). He can also house prisoners in the [[townmasters_hall|town jail]] if needed.

He knows nothing of real value about the wider plot — he's willfully incurious — but he can point to public information like the orc bounty and local geography.
