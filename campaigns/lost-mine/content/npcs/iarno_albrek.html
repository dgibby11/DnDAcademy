Iarno Albrek (Glasstaff)

NPC · Villains

A human wizard in his thirties, once a member of the [[lords_alliance|Lords' Alliance]], now a traitor who has sold his allegiance to the [[nezznar_black_spider|Black Spider]]. He arrived in Phandalin two months ago supposedly to establish order, then founded and took control of the [[redbrands]] instead.

Among the Redbrands he goes by "Glasstaff" — a nickname earned from his elegant glass-and-crystal staff (the [[staff_of_defense]]). No one in Phandalin knows that Glasstaff and Iarno Albrek are the same person — except perhaps [[halia_thornton]], who suspects.

Appearance

Clean-shaven, dark hair swept back, wearing a red-trimmed black robe. Intelligent dark eyes that evaluate everything. He carries himself with the casual superiority of someone who believes he is always the smartest person in the room.

DM Notes

The reveal: The party learns who Glasstaff is via a letter in his quarters (Area 8 of [[redbrand_hideout]]) — a message from the Black Spider naming Iarno directly and outlining his mission. When [[sildar_hallwinter]] learns this, he's quietly devastated — they were friends before Iarno's defection.

Combat: Iarno uses the Mage stat block. His signature tactic is using the [[staff_of_defense]] for Shield before dropping Fireball or Sleep. He will flee via a back tunnel if the party is winning — watch for an escape attempt after he drops below half HP.

If captured: Sildar wants him taken to Neverwinter for trial (Lords' Alliance justice). [[halia_thornton]] wants him for the [[zhentarim]], who will make use of his knowledge. The party's choice here has faction implications. If he escapes, Iarno can resurface later as a more prepared recurring villain.

If questioned: He knows about the Black Spider (a drow named Nezznar), the plan to use Wave Echo Cave's Forge of Spells, and the location of Cragmaw Castle. He'll bargain with this information if he thinks it will save his skin.
