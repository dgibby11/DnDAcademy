Linene Graywind

NPC · Phandalin Locals

The sharp-tongued master of the [[lionshield_coster|Lionshield Coster]], representing the Lionshield merchant company out of Yartar. Linene is a competent, no-nonsense businesswoman in her mid-forties who has no patience for troublemakers — but considerable appreciation for people who solve problems.

She'll offer 50 gp for the return of stolen Lionshield merchandise (crates in [[cragmaw_hideout]], Area 5) and becomes a warm ally if the Redbrands are cleared out.

DM Notes

Linene won't sell weapons to anyone she thinks will use them against Phandalin locals — she's burned by the Redbrands and suspicious. Once the party proves they're legitimate, she extends credit and is willing to hold items for them between adventures. She's a reliable arms supplier for an ongoing Phandalin-based campaign.
