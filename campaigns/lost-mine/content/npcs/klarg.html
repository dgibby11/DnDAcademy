Klarg

NPC · Villains

A bugbear who serves as the leader of the [[cragmaw_hideout]] contingent of the [[cragmaw_tribe|Cragmaw Tribe]]. Klarg is large, aggressive, and obsessed with his own importance. He's been ordered by [[king_grol]] to waylay travelers on Triboar Trail and is taking to the job with enthusiasm.

His second-in-command, [[yeemik]], hates him and is actively plotting to take over.

DM Notes

Klarg is a straightforward combat encounter — he leads with his morningstar and uses his wolf Ripper as a flanking tool. He has 3d8+3 HP and multiattack. He's not smart enough to flee; he fights to the death if cornered.

[[yeemik]] will try to negotiate with the party while Klarg is alive — he wants Klarg dead and will offer [[sildar_hallwinter]] in exchange for the party doing the killing. The party doesn't need to honor that deal after Klarg is dead, but Yeemik becomes hostile if betrayed.

In Klarg's cave: a chest containing 600 cp, 110 sp, 2 ep, a healing potion, and a jade statuette of a frog with golden eyes (40 gp) — all stolen from travelers.
