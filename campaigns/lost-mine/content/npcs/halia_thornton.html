Halia Thornton

NPC · Phandalin Locals

Guildmaster of the [[phandalin_miners_exchange|Phandalin Miner's Exchange]] — a polished, composed woman who watches every conversation with careful intelligence. She keeps impeccable records and speaks in measured, even sentences.

She approaches the party with a proposition: eliminate Glasstaff and bring back any correspondence from his quarters. She'll pay 100 gp.

DM Notes

Halia is a [[zhentarim|Zhentarim]] agent. Her stated reason — to rid Phandalin of the Redbrand threat — is real, if not the whole truth.

Halia wants Glasstaff's letters to learn [[iarno_albrek]]'s criminal contacts and supplier network — so the Zhentarim can step in and run the same operation more smoothly. She's not interested in justice, just in taking over the market Glasstaff was building.

She'll pay 100 gp for the letters regardless of whether Glasstaff is dead or alive (though dead is cleaner for her). If the party hands the letters to [[sildar_hallwinter]] instead, she's disappointed but doesn't retaliate — she's pragmatic and knows when to cut her losses.

Faction play: If the party does her bidding and proves themselves discreet, she recruits them as Zhentarim prospects. Benefits include access to black-market goods, Zhentarim contacts in other cities, and Halia's goodwill. The Zhentarim are morally grey — not evil, but not squeamish.
