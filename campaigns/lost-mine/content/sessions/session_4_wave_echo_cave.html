Session 4: Wave Echo Cave

SESSION · Planning

The climax. The party enters [[wave_echo_cave]] — ancient, vast, and haunted by the dead of a five-hundred-year-old battle. [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar the Black Spider]] is already here with surviving drow and giant spiders. [[nundro_rockseeker]] is his prisoner. The [[flameskull]], [[mormesk]], and [[spectator]] guard their domains. At the center: the [[forge_of_spells|Forge of Spells]], still burning green.

Entering the Cave

Read: "The path narrows to a tunnel carved by water and time. Then the sound hits you — a rhythmic boom, deep as a heartbeat, as if the mountain itself is breathing. Wave Echo Cave." Set the atmosphere: dark, cold, wet, the sound of water always in the background.

The Flameskull

First major threat. It attacks without warning from above. After it's destroyed: "The skull crumbles to ash — but the ash is still warm." Let the party figure out the rejuvenation mechanic. Do they have holy water? Dispel magic? Give them a clock — one hour before it returns.

Mormesk

The wraith floats from the shadows: "You disturb the dead. Name your purpose, or join them." This is a negotiation encounter. He hates the undead here as much as the party does. He hates Nezznar. He wants acknowledgment that he died protecting something worth protecting. Give him that.

The Forge of Spells

Green light ahead — sourceless, cold, beautiful. The [[spectator]] floats before it: "Authorization. State your authorization." Five hundred years of faithful service and the summoner has been dead for centuries. Negotiate, deceive, or fight.

Nezznar

In the northeast section. He's intelligent — he'll hear the party coming if they've made noise. He has [[nundro_rockseeker]] in a corner. "So the Rockseekers sent their errand-runners. You're too late — I've seen the Forge. I understand it now. Nothing you do changes that." He's lying — he needs more time. Fight or negotiate.

Session Goals

  • Navigate Wave Echo Cave and deal with its guardians.
  • Rescue [[nundro_rockseeker]].
  • Defeat or capture [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar]].
  • Secure the [[forge_of_spells|Forge of Spells]].
  • Find [[tharden_rockseeker]]'s body — give [[gundren_rockseeker]] (if present) a moment to grieve.

XP Budget

  • Wave Echo Cave (all encounters): ~3,500 XP
  • Defeating Nezznar: 1,100 XP
  • Rescuing Nundro: 500 XP milestone
  • Securing the Forge: 500 XP milestone
  • Target: Level 5 by end of adventure.

Epilogue

[[gundren_rockseeker]] and [[nundro_rockseeker]] can restart the Forge of Spells. The mine is theirs — the Phandelver Pact reborn. Gundren offers the party a 10% share of all profits going forward, and first access to anything the Forge produces. Phandalin has a future. So does the party.
