Session 2: Trouble in Phandalin

SESSION · Planning

The party has arrived in [[phandalin]] and begins to understand the situation: the [[redbrands]] are terrorizing locals, [[sildar_hallwinter]] wants to establish order, and multiple NPCs have quests and information. The session culminates in clearing the [[redbrand_hideout]] beneath [[tresendar_manor]].

Town Arrival

The wagon draws looks. Redbrand ruffians at the Sleeping Giant watch without moving. [[elmar_barthen]] pays the 10 gp and immediately asks where Gundren is. Suggest the [[stonehill_inn]] for rest.

NPC Circuit

The party can meet: [[toblen_stonehill]] (inn rumors), [[sildar_hallwinter]] (Alliance mission, Glasstaff), [[linene_graywind]] (stolen crates reward), [[sister_garaele]] (Agatha quest), [[daran_edermath]] (Old Owl Well), [[halia_thornton]] (Glasstaff bounty), [[qelline_alderleaf]] (Reidoth, tunnel tip), [[harbin_wester]] (orc bounty). Don't rush — let players drive who they talk to.

Redbrand Encounter

If the party is in town long enough, 4 Redbrand ruffians confront them (especially near the Sleeping Giant or after they ask too many questions). This should escalate into the Hideout.

The Nothic

The [[nothic]] watches from the crevasse. It communicates in whispers. It knows about the letter in Glasstaff's room. It wants meat and acknowledgment. This is the session's best roleplay opportunity.

Glasstaff's Letter

After clearing Area 8: show the party the letter. It names Iarno Albrek. Let them bring it to [[sildar_hallwinter]]. Pause for his reaction. "I know this name."

Session Goals

  • Meet the Phandalin NPCs and collect faction hooks.
  • Clear the Redbrand Hideout; free the Dendrar family.
  • Learn who Glasstaff is (the letter reveal).
  • Establish next steps: Cragmaw Castle to find Gundren? The wilderness sites? Both?

XP Budget

  • Redbrand Hideout (all encounters): ~1,200 XP
  • Freeing prisoners: 200 XP milestone
  • Glasstaff letter reveal: 100 XP milestone
  • Target: ~1,500 XP per player → level 3 after this session.
