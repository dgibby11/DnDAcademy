Session 1: Goblin Arrows

SESSION · Planning

The party is travelling the [[triboar_trail|Triboar Trail]] to Phandalin, delivering a wagon of supplies for [[gundren_rockseeker]]. The session opens with the goblin ambush — dead horses blocking the road, four goblins attacking from the tree line — and ends with the party's first arrival in [[phandalin]].

Opening

Read: "You've spent the last several days travelling east on the Triboar Trail, hauling a wagon of provisions to Phandalin. The sun is warm, the road is rutted, and you're nearly there — when you round a bend and find two horses dead in the road, blocking the way. Before you can react, arrows crack out of the trees."

After Ambush

The goblins that flee leave a trail south into the trees. Examining the horses: they bear the saddlebags of Gundren and Sildar — papers, a letter of introduction to Barthen. Tracks lead south. Players can follow immediately or head to Phandalin first.

Cragmaw Hideout — Entry

Two sentries in the brush; trip wire on the path. The cave smells of wet dog and smoke. Wolves chained near the entrance. Goblins watching from a ledge above.

Yeemik's Deal

Yeemik emerges holding [[sildar_hallwinter]] at knifepoint: "You want human? Kill Klarg. We give you human." He's a goblin who hates his boss and sees an opportunity. The party can deal, bypass him, or attack.

Session Goals

  • Survive the ambush and decide whether to pursue or proceed to Phandalin.
  • Explore Cragmaw Hideout and rescue [[sildar_hallwinter]].
  • Deliver the supply wagon to [[elmar_barthen]] in Phandalin and collect 10 gp.
  • Learn that [[gundren_rockseeker]] was taken to "a castle" — Sildar has more details.

XP Budget

  • 4 goblins (ambush): 200 XP
  • Cragmaw Hideout (all enemies): ~600 XP
  • Rescuing Sildar: 200 XP milestone
  • Delivering wagon: 100 XP milestone
  • Target: ~900–1,100 XP per player → level 2 after this session.
