Session 3: The Spider's Web

SESSION · Planning

The wilderness phase. The party has several leads and can tackle the overland sites in any order: [[ruins_of_thundertree]] (find [[reidoth]], deal with [[venomfang]]), [[cragmaw_castle]] (rescue [[gundren_rockseeker]], find the map), and optional side sites ([[old_owl_well]], [[wyvern_tor]], [[conyberry]]). The session ends with all the information needed to go to [[wave_echo_cave]].

Thundertree

Approach through dead forest. Ash zombies and twig blights patrol. [[reidoth]] shelters in a cottage (Area 4). The dragon's tower looms. [[venomfang]] can be heard shifting weight. Reidoth offers his deal: remove the dragon, get the map to Cragmaw and directions to Wave Echo Cave.

Venomfang Encounter

The dragon is CR 8. Direct combat is probably lethal. Play him as smart and vain. "You enter a dragon's lair and do not bow? Interesting." Diplomacy is the intended path — appeal to his pride, his future, his obvious quality. If they fight: he opens with Poison Breath and retreats to the top of the tower if wounded.

Cragmaw Castle

Hobgoblins at the gatehouse. Multiple entry points. Gundren is in Grol's room. So is the doppelganger — it waits for the right moment to act. The doppelganger can take a party member's face; use this if the fight stalls.

Gundren Rescued

Gundren is injured, grieving (he doesn't know about Tharden yet), and immediately wants to go to the cave. "We have to go. Nundro is still there. The Spider is already there." Give the players a beat to rest first.

Session Goals

  • Deal with Venomfang at Thundertree (combat or diplomacy).
  • Rescue Gundren from Cragmaw Castle.
  • Obtain the location of Wave Echo Cave.
  • Optional: Agatha's quest, Old Owl Well, Wyvern Tor.

XP Budget

  • Cragmaw Castle (all encounters): ~1,800 XP
  • Rescuing Gundren: 500 XP milestone
  • Thundertree site (encounters + Venomfang negotiation): ~500–1,600 XP
  • Target: Level 4 by end of session.
