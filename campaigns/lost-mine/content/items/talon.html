Talon

ITEM · Magic Weapons

A +1 longsword belonging to the late Aldith Tresendar — one of the lords whose family built Tresendar Manor. The blade is engraved with a talon motif along the fuller, and "Talon" is inscribed in Common on the crossguard. The blade still holds its edge despite decades of disuse.

Location: [[redbrand_hideout]], Area 6 (Armory) — buried point-down in a cask of sand, easily overlooked.

Properties

  • +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Longsword (1d8 / 1d10 versatile slashing).
