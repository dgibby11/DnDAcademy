Spider Staff

ITEM · Magic Staves

A black wooden staff capped with a carved obsidian spider, used by [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar the Black Spider]]. The staff has a sinister aesthetic perfectly matched to its owner.

Location: [[wave_echo_cave]], Area 19 — Nezznar's person.

Properties (attunement required, spellcaster)

  • Melee: +1 to attack/damage, 1d6+1 bludgeoning + 1d6 poison.
  • While attuned: Spider Climb spell at will (self only).
  • Charges (10): Web (2 charges), Poison Spray cantrip (0 charges). Regains 1d6+4 charges at dawn.
  • If last charge expended: roll d20 — on 1, the staff crumbles to ash.
