Dragonguard

ITEM · Magic Armor

A +1 breastplate engraved with a motif of a dragon in flight, wings spread across the chest piece. Crafted for a warrior of the Phandelver Pact — one of the defenders who fought in the great battle that sealed Wave Echo Cave.

Location: [[wave_echo_cave]], Area 15 — the Forge of Spells. Mounted on a stand near the forge.

Properties

  • AC 15 + Dex modifier (max 2).
  • +1 bonus to AC.
  • Does not impose disadvantage on Stealth.
