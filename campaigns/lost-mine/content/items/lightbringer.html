Lightbringer

ITEM · Magic Weapons

A +1 mace made for a cleric of Lathander, the god of dawn. The head is shaped like a sunburst and cast in a pale golden metal. When a command word is spoken, it sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet. The light is equivalent to a torch but can be extinguished as a bonus action.

Location: [[wave_echo_cave]], Area 15 — the Forge of Spells. Near the spectator's post.

Properties

  • +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Shedding bright light (20 ft.) on command.
  • Particularly useful against undead in Wave Echo Cave.
