Staff of Defense

ITEM · Magic Staves

A slender staff of pale wood, almost translucent, with a crystal cap that catches light oddly. Used by [[iarno_albrek|Glasstaff]] — hence his nickname. The staff is defensive in nature, well-suited to a wizard who relies on survival rather than raw offensive power.

Location: [[redbrand_hideout]], Area 8 (Glasstaff's quarters) — leaning against the desk.

Properties (attunement required, spellcaster)

  • While holding: +1 bonus to AC.
  • Charges (10): Mage Armor (1 charge), Shield (2 charges). Regains 1d6+4 charges at dawn.
  • If last charge expended: roll d20 — on 1, the staff shatters.
