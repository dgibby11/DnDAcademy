Barthen's Provisions

LOCATION · Phandalin

The largest trading post in Phandalin, run by [[elmar_barthen]]. The shelves are stocked with mining gear, trail rations, torches, rope, lanterns, and most other basic adventuring and frontier supplies. Elmar employs two young clerks, Ander and Thistle.

Notable Stock

  • All standard adventuring gear (PHB prices).
  • Mules and riding horses (on request, 2–3 day wait).
  • No weapons or armour — see [[lionshield_coster]] for those.

DM Notes

The party's first mission is to deliver a wagon of supplies to Elmar on behalf of [[gundren_rockseeker]]. When they arrive, Elmar pays the 10 gp delivery fee and immediately asks: where is Gundren? He's worried — Gundren was supposed to arrive ahead of the wagon and never did.

Elmar knows Gundren was excited about a big discovery in the mines — he mentioned "the old Phandelver mine" but said nothing more. He can direct the party to [[sildar_hallwinter]] if that name comes up.
