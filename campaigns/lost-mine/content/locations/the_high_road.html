The High Road

LOCATION · Wilderness

The principal trade route of the Sword Coast, running north to south along the coastline from Neverwinter through [[phandalin|the Phandalin region]] and south toward Leilon and beyond. Merchants, pilgrims, and caravans use it constantly in good weather; in winter it becomes treacherous where sea wind turns the road to ice.

Where the [[triboar_trail]] branches east off the High Road is the closest waypoint to Phandalin — roughly a day's travel inland from that junction.

DM Notes

The High Road is the party's link to the wider world. Resupply caravans, wandering merchants, and rumors from the north all arrive this way. It's rarely dangerous on the road itself — the real threats wait off it — but an isolated stretch is always a plausible ambush site if you need one.

Travel times from the High Road / Triboar Trail junction: Phandalin ~1 day east; Neverwinter ~3 days north; Leilon ~2 days south.
