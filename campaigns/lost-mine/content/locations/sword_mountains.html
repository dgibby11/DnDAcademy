Sword Mountains

LOCATION · Wilderness

A jagged range of peaks that dominates the southeastern horizon from Phandalin, running roughly north to south and forming a natural barrier between the Sword Coast lowlands and the interior. The mountains are cold, windswept, and dangerous — home to orcs, giants, monsters, and old ruins that have never been fully explored.

The most prominent peak visible from the Triboar Trail region is [[icespire_peak]], its upper flanks perpetually capped in ice.

Somewhere beneath these mountains — its precise location a matter of legend and dispute — lies [[wave_echo_cave]], the source of the Phandelver's Pact.

DM Notes

The Sword Mountains serve as a permanent backdrop and implied threat. Orc raiding parties descend from the range periodically (see [[wyvern_tor]] for the current active band). Giants have been spotted at higher elevation but aren't a session threat in LMoP.

Wave Echo Cave is inside or below the foothills of the Sword Mountains northeast of Phandalin — the exact location is secret until the party finds it.
