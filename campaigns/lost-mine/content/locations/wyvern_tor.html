Wyvern Tor

LOCATION · Wilderness

A prominent rocky outcropping in the hills east of Triboar Trail. An orc war band — a dozen strong, led by an orc named Brughor Axe-Biter — has made camp in a cave on the southeast face. They've been raiding caravans and farms across the region.

DM Notes

[[harbin_wester]] posts a bounty of 500 gp for proof of the orcs' defeat (ten or more ears). The war band is: Brughor Axe-Biter (leader, tougher orc), 1 orog (elite orc), 6 orcs, and an ogre. The cave is straightforward — no tricks, just a fight.

This is a pure combat side encounter, good for a session that needs a straightforward fight. The ogre is the major threat. At this level (3+) it's challenging but manageable with decent tactics. Don't over-telegraph the ogre — let it be a surprise.
