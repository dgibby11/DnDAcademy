Lionshield Coster

LOCATION · Phandalin

A sturdy trading post and warehouse bearing the sign of a blue lion on a white field — the mark of the Lionshields, a merchant company based in Yartar. Run by [[linene_graywind]], a sharp-tongued woman who handles armour, weapons, and heavier trade goods.

Notable Stock

  • Standard weapons and armour (PHB prices).
  • A recent Lionshield shipment was stolen by the Cragmaw goblins — crates are in the cave at [[cragmaw_hideout]]. Returning them earns a 50 gp reward and Linene's lasting goodwill.

DM Notes

[[linene_graywind]] is wary of strangers but will warm up if the party mentions they've had trouble with goblins on Triboar Trail. She'll offer 50 gp for the return of her stolen merchandise (clearly marked crates in area 8 of [[cragmaw_hideout]]).

She knows little about the Redbrands but hates them — they've extorted her twice and she doesn't forgive easily.
