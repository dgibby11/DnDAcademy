Cragmaw Castle

LOCATION · Wilderness

A crumbling castle ruin in the Neverwinter Wood, northeast of Phandalin, occupied by the [[cragmaw_tribe|Cragmaw tribe]] under [[king_grol]]. The castle was built by a wizard-lord centuries ago; only seven of the original towers remain standing, and only partially. The Cragmaws use it as a stronghold and occasionally as a waypoint for their raiding operations.

[[gundren_rockseeker]] is held here as a prisoner, on direct orders from the [[nezznar_black_spider|Black Spider]], who wants Gundren's map to [[wave_echo_cave]].

Approaches

  • Main gate (northwest): Two hobgoblin sentries in the gatehouse towers. Best approached at night or with a distraction.
  • Collapsed section (northeast): A hole in the outer wall; goblins lurk just inside but are less alert.
  • Postern door (south): A side entry used for supply wagons; locked but not guarded on the outside.

Key Areas

  • Gatehouse: Two hobgoblins. Alert. Arrow loops with crossbows.
  • Tower ruins: Various goblin patrols; a doppelganger in disguise working for the Black Spider moves among the inhabitants.
  • Great Hall: Eight goblins eating and arguing. Loud approach brings them out.
  • King Grol's chamber: [[king_grol]] + his dire wolf + a doppelganger. Gundren is here, injured but alive. A map of Phandalin and a note in Undercommon from the Black Spider are on the table.

DM Notes

Cragmaw Castle is the payoff location for Part 3. The party should feel this is a serious dungeon — more organized, more dangerous than the hideout. Hobgoblins are disciplined; goblins are scattered but numerous.

The note from the Black Spider on Grol's table: "I have arranged for the Black Spider to send an emissary [the doppelganger] to retrieve the map. Deliver Gundren Rockseeker to me at Wave Echo Cave. Kill the dwarf's companions." This names [[wave_echo_cave]] and makes clear the Black Spider wants Gundren personally.

[[gundren_rockseeker]] was carrying a map to Wave Echo Cave — it was taken by King Grol. Recovering Gundren AND the map is the goal. Without the map, they still need a clue to find the cave — [[reidoth]] at [[ruins_of_thundertree]] knows the way.

King Grol can be negotiated with (he's pragmatic about survival) but is essentially hostile. The doppelganger should fight to the death — it has an interest in the map reaching the Black Spider.
