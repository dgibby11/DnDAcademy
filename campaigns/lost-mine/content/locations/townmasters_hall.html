Townmaster's Hall

LOCATION · Phandalin

A square stone building serving as both Phandalin's seat of government and its only jail — one crude cell in the back, currently empty. A notice board outside lists postings from the townsmaster and local merchants.

Notice Board

  • Orc Trouble: Harbin Wester posts a 500 gp bounty on orc ears from [[wyvern_tor]] (east of the Triboar Trail). Pays on proof of ten or more orcs slain.

DM Notes — Harbin Wester

[[harbin_wester]] is the elected townmaster. He's fat, pompous, and deeply cowardly. He refuses to acknowledge the Redbrand problem, calling them "harmless ruffians." He's been bribed into silence and knows it.

Once the Redbrands are cleared, Harbin becomes much more helpful — his bribe evaporates and he's pathetically grateful. He can house prisoners and post official bounties. He knows about the orc camp at [[wyvern_tor]] because travelers have been complaining for months; he just hasn't had anyone to send after them.
