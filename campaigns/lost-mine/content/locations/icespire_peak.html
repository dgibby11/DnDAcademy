Icespire Peak

LOCATION · Wilderness

The tallest and most recognisable summit of the [[sword_mountains]], its upper reaches sheathed in permanent ice and visible for miles across the lowlands. A landmark for travelers and a fixture of local legend — the peak has always had a reputation for cold, isolation, and the occasional distant roar that locals attribute to the wind.

From Phandalin, Icespire Peak is visible on clear days to the southeast, a jagged white spire rising above the rest of the range.

DM Notes

In the LMoP adventure, Icespire Peak is a geographical backdrop rather than a destination. Its significance lies in the mountains it anchors — the [[sword_mountains]] range that hides [[wave_echo_cave]] and shelters the orc camps near [[wyvern_tor]].

The peak has a history of draconic activity in D&D lore — Cryovain, a young white dragon, makes Icespire Peak a lair in the follow-up adventure "Dragon of Icespire Peak" (D&D Essentials Kit). Worth keeping in mind if the campaign extends beyond LMoP.
