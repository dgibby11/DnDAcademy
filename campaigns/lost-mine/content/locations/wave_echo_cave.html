Wave Echo Cave

LOCATION · Dungeons

The legendary lost mine of the Phandelver Pact, sealed by a catastrophic battle five hundred years ago. Named for the rhythmic pulse of waves that echo through its vast cavern chambers. The cave holds ancient dwarven and gnomish mine workings, scattered undead remnants of the battle that destroyed it, and — at its heart — the Forge of Spells.

The Black Spider [[nezznar_black_spider]] has arrived here before the party and is conducting his own search, accompanied by a handful of drow and giant spiders.

Key Locations

  • Mine Passages (Areas 1–5): Entry tunnels, collapsed sections, and the body of [[tharden_rockseeker]] (Area 4). [[hew]] lies near the body.
  • Fungi Cavern (Area 6): Poisonous gas pockets; Stirges nesting in the ceiling.
  • Great Cavern (Areas 7–10): Central hub. Old smelter furnace. The [[flameskull]] patrols here.
  • Mormesk's Lair (Area 12): [[mormesk]] the wraith guards a strongbox containing coins and a pouch of gems. Mormesk is the spirit of the last wizard to die here in the great battle.
  • Forge of Spells (Area 15): The magical forge, still operational but guarded by a [[spectator]] bound to protect it. The [[lightbringer]] mace and the [[dragonguard]] breastplate are here.
  • Nezznar's Camp (Area 19): [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar]] with his surviving drow + two giant spiders. [[nundro_rockseeker]] is his prisoner. The [[spider_staff]] is Nezznar's primary weapon.

DM Notes

Wave Echo Cave is the climax of the adventure. It's large and non-linear — the party can approach most areas from multiple directions. The main threats are the [[flameskull]] (which regenerates unless destroyed by radiant or holy water), [[mormesk]] the wraith (incorporeal, life drain), the [[spectator]] at the Forge (which attacks non-authorized visitors), and finally [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar]] himself.

Flameskull: Regenerates to 1 hp after 1 hour unless its ashes are doused with holy water or a dispel magic is cast on them. The party must destroy it properly to prevent it returning.

Mormesk: Can be negotiated with — he was a mage who died here and resents being turned into a wraith. He loathes the undead monsters that now infest his cave. He might stand aside or even provide information if treated with respect. He wants his memory honoured, not his strongbox raided.

Spectator: Has been guarding the Forge for 500 years; its summoner is long dead, but it hasn't noticed. It attacks anyone approaching the Forge without "authorization." The party can attempt to deceive it (Deception DC 15) or simply fight. After combat it realizes its contract has lapsed and departs peacefully.

Nezznar: His goal is to activate the Forge of Spells and use it to create powerful magic items for the drow. He will negotiate briefly if cornered, but he's fundamentally hostile — he ordered the murder of [[tharden_rockseeker]] and the capture of [[nundro_rockseeker]] and [[gundren_rockseeker]]. If defeated (but not killed), he'll barter information for his life.

Forge of Spells: The forge can be restarted with the right knowledge (see [[fate_of_phandelver_pact]]). It can enchant one non-magical weapon or armor per day to +1. This is a major reward for completing the adventure — a reason for the party to maintain the mine going forward.
