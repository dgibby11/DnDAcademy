Edermath Orchard

LOCATION · Phandalin

A pleasant apple orchard and cottage on the western edge of Phandalin, home to [[daran_edermath]], a retired adventurer. The orchard is well-kept and productive. Daran is a friendly, direct man who clearly knows his way around a sword, despite the cider press and the apple trees.

Quest Hook: Old Owl Well

[[daran_edermath]] is an agent of the [[order_of_the_gauntlet]]. He has heard that a wizard in red robes has been digging around [[old_owl_well]], raising undead and bothering local miners. He asks the party to investigate and put a stop to it.

Reward: Daran sponsors the party's induction into the Order of the Gauntlet (if they're interested), granting the faction benefit. He also has useful information about Neverwinter Wood if they mention Cragmaw Castle.
