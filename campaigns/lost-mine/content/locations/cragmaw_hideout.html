Cragmaw Hideout

LOCATION · Wilderness

A cave complex hidden in a hillside a few miles off Triboar Trail, concealed by thick thorn scrub. This is where the party is meant to be headed in Part 1 — tracking the goblins who ambushed them on the road to Phandalin and who hold [[sildar_hallwinter]] prisoner.

Approach & Traps

  • Two goblin sentries lurk in the brush 90 feet from the cave mouth (Perception DC 12 to spot).
  • A trip wire of empty cans stretches across the path inside the tree line (Perception DC 12, disarmed with DC 10 Dex).
  • A second wire further in triggers a collapse of logs (Perception DC 15).

Key Areas

  • Cave Mouth (Area 1): Two wolves chained up. Calm with DC 13 Animal Handling; attack if strangers rush past.
  • Goblin Blind (Area 2): Three goblins watching the entrance from a natural shelf 20 ft up. A stream channel runs through the floor.
  • Kennel (Area 3): Three wolves. Freed goblins might release them as a distraction.
  • Flood Sluice (Area 4): Goblins can release a torrent to knock intruders down; happens twice before the basin empties.
  • Goblin Den (Area 5): Six goblins. Stolen crates from [[lionshield_coster]] stacked here (clearly marked). [[yeemik]] is here, plotting against [[klarg]].
  • Klarg's Cave (Area 6): [[klarg]] the bugbear and his wolf Ripper. A chest contains stolen coin + potions. Sildar is tied up here.

DM Notes

This is a classic goblin cave — lots of verticality, the flood trap, sneaking/negotiating vs. fighting. Encourage players to use the terrain.

[[yeemik]] will offer to betray [[klarg]] if the party spares him — he wants Klarg's head (literally). He'll hand over [[sildar_hallwinter]] in exchange. Yeemik is not a long-term ally; he'll turn on the party when it's convenient, but the immediate deal can work.

[[sildar_hallwinter]] was captured when the goblins ambushed him and [[gundren_rockseeker]] on the road. Gundren was taken elsewhere — to [[cragmaw_castle]] — by special order of the Black Spider. Sildar knows this (he overheard the goblins discussing it).

Lionshield crates: mark them as clearly labeled "Lionshield Coster, Phandalin." The party can earn 50 gp from [[linene_graywind]] for returning them.
