Stonehill Inn

LOCATION · Phandalin

A sturdy common room with a few private sleeping quarters above. Run by [[toblen_stonehill]] and his wife Trilena. The common room is the social hub of Phandalin — miners drink here after their shifts, travelers share road news, and locals mutter about Redbrand trouble when they think no one is listening.

Services

  • Private room: 5 sp/night. Common room floor: 1 sp/night.
  • Hearty meals: 3 sp.
  • Ale: 4 cp/mug.

Rumors (overheard at the bar)

  • Pip Stonehill (Toblen's son): "There's a wizard who lives in the old manor. A real mean one."
  • Elsa (barmaid): Orcs have been raiding along Triboar Trail. The Rockseeker brothers rented rooms here — haven't been seen in weeks.
  • Lanar (miner): Redbrand ruffians have been making problems. Townmaster does nothing.
  • Freda (weaver): Redbrands murdered the woodcutter Thel Dendrar and kidnapped his family.

DM Notes

The Stonehill Inn is the default "home base" for the party. [[sildar_hallwinter]] stays here once rescued from [[cragmaw_hideout]]. The rumors above are all hooks — feel free to let the party hear 1–2 per evening spent here.

Toblen is friendly but cautious; he's been shaken down once by the Redbrands and doesn't want trouble. He'll share rumors freely once the party has cleared the Redbrands.
