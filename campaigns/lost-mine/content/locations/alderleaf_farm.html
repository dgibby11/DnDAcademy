Alderleaf Farm

LOCATION · Phandalin

A tidy halfling farmstead on the southern edge of Phandalin. [[qelline_alderleaf]] runs it alone since her husband died, with the help of her nephew Carp. The farm grows herbs, vegetables, and keeps a handful of goats. It's one of the most peaceful spots in an otherwise tense town.

Quest Hook: Reidoth the Druid

[[qelline_alderleaf]] knows a druid named [[reidoth]] who travels the region. She believes Reidoth knows every inch of the wilderness north and east of Phandalin — including, perhaps, the location of [[wave_echo_cave]]. She suggests the party look for him at the ruins of [[ruins_of_thundertree]].

Carp, her nephew, has seen a secret tunnel entrance near [[tresendar_manor]] — he spotted Redbrands using it and can show the party if they ask nicely and promise not to get him in trouble.
