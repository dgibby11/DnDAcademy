Old Owl Well

LOCATION · Wilderness

A ruined watchtower from the old empire of Netheril, set in the hills northeast of Phandalin. The well still draws clean water. A Red Wizard of Thay — [[hamun_kost]] — has pitched a tent here and set a ring of twelve zombies to guard the perimeter while he excavates the ruins.

DM Notes

[[hamun_kost]] is not immediately hostile. He's focused on archaeological research — looking for old Netherese magic. He'll send the zombies after anyone who approaches aggressively, but if the party opens with diplomacy he'll listen.

Kost's quest: he wants information about the necromancer [[mormesk]] who supposedly died in [[wave_echo_cave]] during the great battle. He'll let the party use the well and stand down his zombies in exchange for a promise to bring back any information about Mormesk's death — a name, a detail, anything. If they later return with info, he pays 150 gp in gems.

This is a low-stakes encounter useful for teaching players that "kill everything" isn't always the right approach. Kost is a Red Wizard doing something slightly shady but not actively harmful right now.
