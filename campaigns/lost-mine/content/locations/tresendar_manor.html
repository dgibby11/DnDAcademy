Tresendar Manor

LOCATION · Phandalin

Burned-out ruins on a low hill at the eastern end of Phandalin. The walls still stand but the roof collapsed long ago. The place has sat derelict for decades. Recently, the [[redbrands]] have taken it as their turf — and more importantly, as access to the cellars below.

The cellar beneath the manor serves as the [[redbrand_hideout|Redbrand Hideout]], a complex of natural caves and carved stone rooms. Access is via a trapdoor hidden under debris in the ruined main hall, or through a tunnel behind a secret door that Carp Alderleaf has spotted (see [[alderleaf_farm]]).

DM Notes

The manor ruins are the threshold to Part 2 of the adventure. The party may stumble onto it by following Redbrands, being tipped off by locals, or getting ambushed on the road and deciding to hit back at the source.

The ruins themselves are not populated — the action is below in the [[redbrand_hideout]].
