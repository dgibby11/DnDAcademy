Phandalin Miner's Exchange

LOCATION · Phandalin

The Exchange serves as a claim registry and ore clearinghouse for the prospectors who work the hills around Phandalin. Run by [[halia_thornton]], an elegant, reserved woman who keeps meticulous records and doesn't miss a thing.

Halia is polite, measured, and offers the party a very reasonable proposition: find and eliminate Glasstaff, the Redbrand leader, and bring back any correspondence he keeps. She offers 100 gp.

DM Notes

[[halia_thornton]] is a [[zhentarim]] agent. Her actual interest in Glasstaff's correspondence is to identify [[iarno_albrek]]'s contacts and supply lines so the Zhentarim can absorb the Redbrand operation — not because she wants justice.

She won't reveal her Zhentarim affiliation unless the party joins. She pays the 100 gp if they bring the letters; she's disappointed but not hostile if they hand the letters to [[sildar_hallwinter]] instead.

If the party proves themselves: Halia can recruit them as Zhentarim prospects, granting the faction benefit (see [[zhentarim]]).
