Ruins of Thundertree

LOCATION · Wilderness

A ruined town at the foot of Mount Hotenow, destroyed thirty years ago when the volcano erupted. Ash zombies wander the empty streets; twig blights infest the overgrown gardens. At the center, a stone tower — once the home of a wizard — now shelters [[venomfang]], a young green dragon who moved in recently and finds the ruins a convenient base.

The druid [[reidoth]] also lurks here, watching Venomfang carefully and trying to understand what drew the dragon to Thundertree.

Points of Interest

  • Dragon's Tower (Area 7): [[venomfang]]'s lair. Treasure hoard: 800 cp, 2,500 sp, 180 gp, an ornate drinking horn (250 gp), two spell scrolls, a + 1 shield. The dragon is clever and can negotiate.
  • Herbalist's Shop (Area 9): Mirna Dendrar's family shop — contains [[mirnas_heirloom]] (emerald necklace, 200 gp) behind the counter in a stone box.
  • Reidoth's Shelter (Area 4): [[reidoth]] camps here. He'll meet the party if approached peacefully.

DM Notes

Thundertree serves multiple purposes: it's the location of [[reidoth]] (who knows where [[cragmaw_castle]] is and can guide them to [[wave_echo_cave]]), the site of [[mirnas_heirloom]], and home to [[venomfang]] (a potentially deadly encounter that rewards diplomacy).

Venomfang: The dragon is CR 8 — a direct fight at this level is suicidal. But Venomfang is young and somewhat vain. He might negotiate: leave Thundertree alone if the party brings him "a worthy gift" (he wants something from a dragon's hoard, not junk). He might also let them go if they can convince him Thundertree is beneath his potential. If he decides they're insulting him, he attacks.

Reidoth's quests: (1) Find the location of Cragmaw Castle — Reidoth has been watching it and will share the coordinates if the party drives Venomfang away. (2) He can also lead the party to Wave Echo Cave once the dragon is gone or neutralized.
