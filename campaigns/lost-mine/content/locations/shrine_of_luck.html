Shrine of Luck

LOCATION · Phandalin

A small stone building dedicated to Tymora, goddess of luck. The shrine is simple — a smooth altar stone, a few coin offerings, and a handful of dried flowers that someone has recently placed. Tended by [[sister_garaele]], an acolyte of the Harpers.

Quest: Bowgentle's Spellbook

[[sister_garaele]] has been tasked by the Harpers to recover a spellbook that once belonged to a mage named Bowgentle. The book is now sought by [[agatha|Agatha the Banshee]], who haunts the ruins near [[conyberry]]. Garaele tried to barter with Agatha for information about the book but was driven away. She asks the party to visit Agatha with a gift — a jeweled comb — and ask where Bowgentle's spellbook is.

Reward: Three potions of healing. If the party succeeds, Garaele also gives them access to Harper contacts and training opportunities (see [[harpers]]).

What Agatha knows: She sold the spellbook to a necromancer named Tsernoth from Iriaebor, over a century ago. The book is long gone.
