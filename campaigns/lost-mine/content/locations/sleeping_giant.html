Sleeping Giant Tap House

LOCATION · Phandalin

A low-roofed, poorly lit tavern at the eastern end of town, frequented almost entirely by [[redbrands|Redbrand Ruffians]]. A mean-looking half-ogre named Grista runs the bar. The Redbrands lounge here between their extortion rounds, and they don't appreciate strangers asking questions.

If the party enters here before dealing with the Redbrands, four ruffians pick a fight. They're spoiling for an excuse. Grista watches indifferently — she doesn't care who wins, just that she gets paid.

DM Notes

The Sleeping Giant is a flavor location more than a resource. It's useful for putting the Redbrand problem in the party's face early. If they wipe out four ruffians in broad daylight, the remaining Redbrands hear about it and become more aggressive — or more frightened, depending on how you play it.

After the [[redbrand_hideout]] is cleared, Grista goes back to serving anyone who pays. She's not loyal to the Redbrands, just indifferent.
