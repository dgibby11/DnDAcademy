Triboar Trail

LOCATION · Wilderness

A rutted dirt road running roughly east-west through the Sword Coast wilderness, connecting the town of Triboar to the coast road near Neverwinter. It's the main overland route through this part of the region and the fastest path between Neverwinter and inland destinations like Phandalin.

The trail has become increasingly dangerous. [[cragmaw_tribe|Cragmaw goblins]] prey on travelers, and orc bands have been spotted east of the hills. There are no inns along the road — travelers camp rough or push through.

Wilderness Encounters

The DM may roll for random encounters when the party travels the trail. Key fixed locations off the trail:

  • [[cragmaw_hideout]] — 2 miles south of the ambush site, following goblin tracks.
  • [[old_owl_well]] — Northeast, in the hills.
  • [[wyvern_tor]] — East of the trail, a half-day's journey from Phandalin.
  • [[ruins_of_thundertree]] — North, near Mount Hotenow.
  • [[conyberry]] — East of the trail, 2 days from Phandalin.
  • [[cragmaw_castle]] — Northeast, in Neverwinter Wood.

DM Notes

The adventure begins on Triboar Trail. The party is escorting a wagon for [[gundren_rockseeker]] to Phandalin when they are ambushed by Cragmaw goblins. Read the boxed text from the adventure book for the ambush scene.

Ambush setup: Horses are dead and blocking the road. Four goblins attack from the trees; more flee back toward the hideout. Sildar's body is NOT here — he was taken earlier. The party can find goblin tracks leading south toward [[cragmaw_hideout]].
