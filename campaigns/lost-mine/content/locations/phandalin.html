Phandalin

LOCATION · Towns

A small frontier town of perhaps 500 residents, Phandalin sits on the remains of a much older settlement that was sacked by orcs long ago. The town has only recently been resettled, attracting miners, farmers, and merchants looking to claim a share of the region's modest prosperity.

The town consists of a handful of dirt-track streets lined with timber and rough-stone buildings. There is no wall, no garrison — only the half-hearted authority of Townmaster Harbin Wester and the private violence of a gang called the Redbrand Ruffians, who have made the eastern quarter their domain.

DM Notes

Phandalin is the campaign's home base. The party will likely return here between adventure sites. Use the NPCs here to dispense quests and faction hooks — each major NPC represents a different faction of the five major factions.

Redbrand situation: The Redbrands number about 15 total (1 doppelganger, 2 bugbears, 1 nothic, 10 ruffians). Clearing the hideout (Part 2) dissolves them as a threat. If [[iarno_albrek]] escapes, he may reappear later as a recurring villain.

Faction hooks: [[sister_garaele]] (Harpers) → [[bowgentles_spellbook]]; [[daran_edermath]] (Order) → [[old_owl_well]]; [[halia_thornton]] (Zhentarim) → capture Glasstaff alive; [[harbin_wester]] → orc bounty at [[wyvern_tor]]; [[sildar_hallwinter]] (Lords' Alliance) → find [[iarno_albrek]] and [[cragmaw_castle]].
