DM Notes

Goblins in the Cragmaw Hideout use Nimble Escape constantly — they hit-and-run, duck behind stalagmites, and force the party to chase them. Don't play them as standing still. They know every corner of the cave and will try to funnel the party into chokepoints where the party can't use their numbers advantage.

The ambush on Triboar Trail: 4 goblins, 2 already dead at the scene (slain by Sildar's efforts before capture). The survivors attack from the brush on both sides of the road.

Typical cave encounter group: 3–5 goblins. They have a horn to sound alarm — if they use it, add a wave of 2d4 more goblins from deeper in the cave after 1 round.

The wolves in the kennel area (see [[wolf]]) are chained and can be freed by goblins in an emergency — takes a bonus action on a goblin's turn.
