Nothic

CREATURE · Named Creatures

A warped, one-eyed creature that lurks in the crevasse beneath [[redbrand_hideout|Tresendar Manor's cellars]]. Once a wizard, the nothic was transformed by forbidden Vecna-related arcane knowledge. [[iarno_albrek|Glasstaff]] found it here and made an arrangement: he leaves scraps of meat, and it keeps watch on the crevasse and uses its Weird Insight to gather secrets about the Redbrands' prisoners.

Tactics

  • CR 2 — dangerous primarily due to its Rotting Gaze.
  • Claws: +4 to hit, 1d6+3 slashing × 2.
  • Rotting Gaze: 30-ft. range, DC 12 Con save or 3d6 necrotic (magical rot).
  • Weird Insight: contested Perception (nothic) vs. Deception (target) — learns one secret about the target on success.

DM Notes

The nothic is the most interesting NPC encounter in the Redbrand Hideout. It can be negotiated with — it's intelligent, though disturbing. It communicates through telepathy and prefers to be treated as a peer rather than a monster.

It knows: (1) about the letter in Glasstaff's room, (2) that [[talon]] is in the armory barrel, (3) various secrets it has gleaned from prisoners and Redbrands via Weird Insight. It will share this information in exchange for food (meat), for being left alone, or for the promise that the party won't destroy Glasstaff's operation without a better offer for the nothic.

If the party attacks it immediately, it fights to defend itself. It has no particular loyalty to Glasstaff — only to continued survival and occasional feeding. It might linger in the crevasse even after the Redbrands are gone, becoming a grotesque but potentially useful contact for future adventures.
