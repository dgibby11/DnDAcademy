Traits

Keen Hearing and Smell. The wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Pack Tactics. The wolf has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is adjacent to the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

Actions

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (2d4+2) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

DM Notes

Three wolves are chained in the kennel area of [[cragmaw_hideout]]. They are half-starved and hostile to anyone who isn't a goblin. [[klarg]]'s wolf Ripper runs loose with him in the final cave — Ripper has the same stat block.

Pack Tactics triggers as soon as any goblin is adjacent to the target. In a mixed encounter with goblins and wolves, the wolves should go for prone targets immediately — a prone creature grants advantage on melee attacks against it, stacking with Pack Tactics for near-guaranteed hits.

Chained wolves: a DC 12 Animal Handling check made as an action can calm a chained wolf. Releasing a chained wolf requires a key (one goblin guard has it) or a DC 12 Dexterity (thieves' tools) check. Freed wolves without a handler have a 50% chance of attacking the nearest creature regardless of side.
