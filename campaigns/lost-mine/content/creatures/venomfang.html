Venomfang

CREATURE · Named Creatures

A young green dragon who moved into the ruined wizard's tower at [[ruins_of_thundertree]] a few months ago, displacing whatever was already there. Venomfang is young — perhaps fifty years old — and has not yet established a proper lair or hoard. He's been accumulating wealth slowly, and Thundertree's ruins offer defensible shelter while he hunts the surrounding forest.

Tactics

  • CR 8 — severely dangerous for a level 3–4 party in direct combat.
  • Poison Breath (recharge 5–6): 30-ft. cone, 12d6 poison damage (DC 16 Con save halves).
  • Multiattack: bite + two claws.
  • Amphibious: can breathe air and water.
  • Prefers to open with Poison Breath, then withdraw to a safe position.

DM Notes

Venomfang is not immediately hostile if approached with clear intent to talk. He's arrogant and vain — a green dragon's defining trait. He sees the party as interesting, possibly useful, possibly a meal. How the conversation starts shapes what happens next.

He can be persuaded to leave Thundertree if: (a) given a gift worthy of a dragon's hoard (something from another dragon's hoard, a rare gemstone, or an item of genuine magical significance), or (b) convinced that Thundertree is beneath his potential — that a dragon of his obvious quality deserves a more prestigious lair. His ego can be weaponized against him.

If the party fights him: it should feel genuinely dangerous. One or two Poison Breath uses at this level can end an encounter. Consider having him flee at half HP if the party is clearly winning — he's young and pragmatic enough to retreat. He'll remember the party and hold a grudge.

Treasure hoard: 800 cp, 2,500 sp, 180 gp, an ornate drinking horn worth 250 gp, two spell scrolls (Darkness and Invisibility), and a +1 shield (Dragonguard's twin — same maker).
