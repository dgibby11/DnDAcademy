Flameskull

CREATURE · Named Creatures

A floating skull wreathed in green fire, patrolling the smelter cavern and adjacent passages of [[wave_echo_cave]]. Created by the wizards of the Phandelver Pact to guard the mine — it has continued its patrol for five hundred years without deviation.

Tactics

  • CR 4 — dangerous primarily due to its regeneration and fire spells.
  • Fire Ray (×3/turn): ranged spell attack +5, 3d6 fire damage.
  • Spells: Fireball (DC 13 Dex, 8d6 fire), Flaming Sphere, Shield, Blur, Mage Armor.
  • Rejuvenation: 1 hour after being destroyed, regains 1 HP and recovers — UNLESS its ashes are doused with holy water or a dispel magic is cast on the remains.

DM Notes

The flameskull's Rejuvenation is the defining tactical challenge. Make sure the party encounters its remains and has a chance to discover the mechanic — let them try to destroy the remains and figure out they need holy water or dispel magic. If they don't know this, the flameskull is back in an hour.

It doesn't speak or negotiate — it's a magical construct running on ancient instructions. It attacks all intruders without exception. The party cannot reason with it.

Once the cave is cleared of threats, the flameskull can theoretically continue its guard function on behalf of new owners (the party, the Rockseekers). It's not malicious — just very literal about its programming.
