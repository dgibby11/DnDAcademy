The Phandelver Pact

MYSTERY · Lore Mysteries

Five hundred years ago, the human spellcasters of the Phandelver Pact struck an agreement with dwarf and gnome miners: access to the dwarves' tunnels in exchange for arcane expertise. Together they built [[wave_echo_cave|Wave Echo Cave]] into a thriving operation centered on the Forge of Spells — a magical furnace that could enchant weapons and armor.

Then orcs attacked. The battle was catastrophic. The mine collapsed. The location was lost. All three of the families who held the secret — including the Rockseekers — kept looking for it across generations.

What Happened in the Battle

  • Orc forces overran the mine entrance. Gnome and dwarf defenders fought a desperate rearguard action inside the cave.
  • The mages, including [[mormesk]], triggered a magical explosion that collapsed the tunnels — trapping attackers and defenders alike.
  • The mine fell silent. The Pact died with it.

DM Notes

The Phandelver Pact is background lore that enriches the cave's atmosphere. The key revelation for play is that the [[forge_of_spells|Forge of Spells]] still works — meaning the party and the Rockseekers can reactivate it. This transforms the adventure from "stop the villain" to "reclaim a lost legacy," which is a much more satisfying arc.

The Forge's reactivation is the epilogue hook: [[gundren_rockseeker]] and [[nundro_rockseeker]] can restart it with the party's help. The new Phandelver Pact — the party, the Rockseekers, and whoever else they bring in — is the seed of further adventures.
