Where is Wave Echo Cave?

MYSTERY · Primary Mysteries

The location of [[wave_echo_cave|Wave Echo Cave]] — the lost mine of the Phandelver Pact — is the central mystery driving the adventure. [[gundren_rockseeker]] found it, mapped it, and was captured before he could share that knowledge. Now the party must recover the information.

Known Clues

  • [[sildar_hallwinter]] knows Gundren was heading toward Phandalin with a map to an old mine — but doesn't know the mine's location himself.
  • [[elmar_barthen]] heard Gundren mention "the old Phandelver mine" but nothing specific.
  • [[qelline_alderleaf]] says [[reidoth]] the druid knows the land better than anyone.

How to Resolve

  • Path A: Rescue [[gundren_rockseeker]] from [[cragmaw_castle]] — he memorized the location and can lead the party there.
  • Path B: Recover Gundren's map from [[king_grol|King Grol's]] chamber in Cragmaw Castle.
  • Path C: Ask [[reidoth]] at [[ruins_of_thundertree]] — he knows the cave's location and will share it after the party helps with [[venomfang]].
