Who is Glasstaff?

MYSTERY · Primary Mysteries

The mysterious leader of the [[redbrands|Redbrand Ruffians]], known only by his nickname — earned from the distinctive glass-and-crystal staff he carries. He controls the gang from somewhere beneath [[tresendar_manor]] and has been terrorizing Phandalin for months. [[sildar_hallwinter]] arrived in Phandalin partly to find a missing colleague and would be very interested to know who Glasstaff is.

Clues

  • Redbrand ruffians know him as Glasstaff — few have seen him directly.
  • The [[nothic]] in the [[redbrand_hideout|Redbrand Hideout]] knows a great deal and may hint at secrets.
  • The letter in Area 8 of the Redbrand Hideout names him directly: Iarno Albrek.

DM Notes — The Reveal

Glasstaff is [[iarno_albrek|Iarno Albrek]], a former member of the [[lords_alliance|Lords' Alliance]] who was sent to Phandalin two months ago and turned instead to the Black Spider's employ. [[sildar_hallwinter]] was here partly looking for Iarno — they're old friends.

This reveal works best when the party brings the letter to Sildar. His reaction — genuine shock, then grief, then grim resolution — is the emotional payoff. Don't rush it. Let Sildar read it, go quiet for a moment, then confirm: "I know this name. Iarno was my friend."
