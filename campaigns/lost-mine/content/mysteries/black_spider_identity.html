Who is the Black Spider?

MYSTERY · Primary Mysteries

A shadowy figure who has been directing the [[cragmaw_tribe]] and controlling [[iarno_albrek|Glasstaff]]'s Redbrand operation from afar. Goblins talk about "the Spider" in hushed tones. The Redbrands refer to Glasstaff's employer the same way. But no one local has seen the Black Spider directly.

Clues

  • [[cragmaw_hideout]]: Goblins mention Gundren was taken at "the Spider's" specific request.
  • [[redbrand_hideout]], Area 8: A letter from the Black Spider names [[iarno_albrek]] and outlines his mission — it's signed with a spider symbol, no name.
  • [[cragmaw_castle]]: A note in [[king_grol]]'s room references the Black Spider and mentions Wave Echo Cave by name.
  • [[wave_echo_cave]]: The Black Spider is there in person when the party arrives.

DM Notes — The Reveal

The Black Spider is [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar]], a drow wizard. The party will likely not encounter him until Wave Echo Cave — by then they'll have built up a picture of someone ruthless and organized. The reveal of a drow is deliberately anticlimactic if the party was hoping for something more elaborate — Nezznar is just a drow with a goal. The simplicity is the point.

The most dramatic reveal moment is in [[cragmaw_castle]] — if the party finds the note in Grol's room that explicitly says the Black Spider wants Gundren brought to Wave Echo Cave, they know the cave is the endgame and that the Spider is already there.
