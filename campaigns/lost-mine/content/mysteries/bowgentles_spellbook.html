Bowgentle's Spellbook

MYSTERY · Side Quests

A Harpers quest for [[sister_garaele]]. The organization wants to recover a spellbook belonging to a mage named Bowgentle — a figure of some historical significance. The last known holder of the book was [[agatha|Agatha the Banshee]], who lurks near [[conyberry]]. Garaele tried to negotiate with Agatha directly and was driven off.

The party is asked to approach Agatha with a gift — a jeweled silver comb — and ask what became of Bowgentle's spellbook.

Resolution

Agatha's answer: she sold the spellbook to a necromancer named Tsernoth from Iriaebor, more than a century ago. The book is lost — Tsernoth is long dead and his tower fell. This is a dead end, and Sister Garaele knows it when the party reports back. She thanks them anyway and provides the promised reward (three potions of healing).

The mystery's real value is the visit to [[agatha|Agatha]] — a memorable roleplaying encounter — and cementing the party's relationship with the [[harpers|Harpers]] through [[sister_garaele]].
