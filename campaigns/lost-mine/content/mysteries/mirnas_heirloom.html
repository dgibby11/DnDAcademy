Mirna's Heirloom

MYSTERY · Side Quests

Among the prisoners in the [[redbrand_hideout|Redbrand Hideout]] is Mirna Dendrar, kidnapped along with her children after the Redbrands murdered her husband Thel. Once freed, Mirna is desperate to help — but has no money. Instead, she tells the party about something she left behind.

Before fleeing [[ruins_of_thundertree]] thirty years ago during the eruption, her family hid a family heirloom in the herb and alchemy shop they ran. In a stone box behind the counter: an emerald necklace worth 200 gp.

DM Notes

This is a simple treasure-recovery side quest that rewards exploring Thundertree — specifically the herbalist's shop (Area 9), which the party will already be visiting if they're looking for [[reidoth]] or dealing with [[venomfang]]. The necklace is there if they look behind the counter. No combat required to retrieve it (though twig blights and ash zombies patrol nearby).

The emotional payoff is returning it to Mirna — she's genuinely moved. If the party keeps it, nothing mechanically happens, but it's a missed opportunity for a moment of genuine gratitude.
