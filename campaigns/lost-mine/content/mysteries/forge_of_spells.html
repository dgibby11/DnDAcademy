The Forge of Spells

MYSTERY · Lore Mysteries

At the heart of [[wave_echo_cave]] stands the Forge of Spells — a great hearth of magical fire built by the gnome mages of the Phandelver Pact. In its heyday, the forge could imbue weapons and armor with permanent enchantments. It was the Pact's greatest secret and the reason the orcs attacked.

The forge still burns with a cold green light, five centuries after the battle. The [[spectator]] guards it. What it could mean for the future is the real question.

DM Notes

The Forge of Spells is the campaign's ultimate reward — more significant than any treasure hoard. With [[nundro_rockseeker]]'s engineering knowledge and [[gundren_rockseeker]]'s connections, the party can reactivate it as a functioning operation.

Mechanical reward: The Forge can enchant one weapon or piece of armor per day to +1 (or improve a +1 item to +2 with a full week of work). This is Gundren's promised "10% share" made tangible — the party has access to a magic item manufactory.

Why [[nezznar_black_spider|Nezznar]] wants it: A working Forge of Spells in drow hands would be transformative — not just for Nezznar personally but for whoever controlled the output. His plan is to monopolize it, not share it. This is why he's been so aggressive about clearing the surface threats and securing the cave quietly.
