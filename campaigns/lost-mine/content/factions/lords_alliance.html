Lords' Alliance

FACTION · Major Factions

A coalition of rulers from across the Sword Coast and the North — Baldur's Gate, Neverwinter, Waterdeep, and others — who cooperate to maintain stability and mutual defense. The Alliance isn't idealistic; it's pragmatic. Its members want trade routes protected, borders respected, and threats to civilization dealt with before they grow too large to handle.

Local representative: [[sildar_hallwinter]].

What They Want in Phandalin

  • Locate [[iarno_albrek]], who was sent to establish order and vanished.
  • Clear threats to the trade road (goblins, orcs, Redbrands).
  • Ensure Phandalin remains stable and under legitimate authority.

Joining the Alliance

Sildar Hallwinter can sponsor a party member into the Lords' Alliance. Benefit: access to Alliance contacts, letters of credit with Alliance-member city merchants, and official status as a recognized agent of order.
