Order of the Gauntlet

FACTION · Major Factions

A devout and vigilant military order dedicated to protecting the innocent from evil. Where the Lords' Alliance is political and the Harpers are covert, the Order is direct — they identify threats, confront them, and destroy them. Members include paladins, clerics, and warriors who have sworn oaths against specific evils.

Local representative: [[daran_edermath]].

What They Want in Phandalin

  • Investigate the undead activity at [[old_owl_well]].
  • Protect Phandalin from evil threats.

Joining the Order

Daran Edermath can sponsor a party member who demonstrates courage and lawful motivation. Benefit: access to Order temples (safe rest, healing magic), patrol network information, and the Order's seal (which grants social standing with lawful organizations).
