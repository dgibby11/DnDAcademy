Cragmaw Tribe

FACTION · Antagonists

A large goblin tribe led by bugbears, operating out of two bases: [[cragmaw_hideout]] (a cave on Triboar Trail) and [[cragmaw_castle]] in Neverwinter Wood. The tribe raids caravans, extracts tolls from travelers, and provides muscle for hire to those willing to pay — including the [[nezznar_black_spider|Black Spider]], who has been paying them well.

Leadership: [[king_grol]] (overall chief, Cragmaw Castle) and [[klarg]] (lieutenant, Cragmaw Hideout). [[yeemik]] plots against Klarg.

DM Notes

The Cragmaws are not a monolithic force — they're a loosely organized band held together by Grol's authority and the Black Spider's gold. When Klarg is killed, the hideout contingent fragments. When Grol is killed, the castle contingent collapses — the remaining goblins scatter into the forest rather than fight for a dead chief.

The tribe has about 40–50 goblins and 6–8 bugbears in total, spread across both locations. They're not a faction that can be negotiated with as a group — individual members might deal, but the tribe itself has no political structure beyond raw dominance.
