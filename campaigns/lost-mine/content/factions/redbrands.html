Redbrand Ruffians

FACTION · Antagonists

A criminal gang that has taken over Phandalin's eastern quarter, run by [[iarno_albrek|Glasstaff]] from a base in the cellars beneath [[tresendar_manor]]. The Redbrands shake down merchants, intimidate locals, and have kidnapped at least one family. They take their name from the red cloaks they all wear.

DM Notes

The gang numbers about fifteen: ten human ruffians, two bugbears, a doppelganger, and the [[nothic]] that Glasstaff has domesticated in the crevasse below.

The Redbrands are thugs, not soldiers — they rely on fear and numbers, not discipline. Without Glasstaff, they collapse. Most ruffians will surrender if Glasstaff is captured/killed and they're clearly outnumbered. Three or four might flee Phandalin entirely.

The gang's real purpose in the plot is as [[nezznar_black_spider|the Black Spider's]] proxy in Phandalin — they were supposed to run off anyone who got too curious about the mine. Glasstaff let them get too visible and too violent, which has now attracted exactly the kind of attention the Black Spider wanted to avoid.

Individual members of note: Mosk and Thresh are the two bugbears (tougher than the human ruffians). The doppelganger pretends to be a human ruffian named "Vyerith" — it's gathering information for the Black Spider and doesn't care about the Redbrands' survival.
