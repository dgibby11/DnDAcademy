Emerald Enclave

FACTION · Major Factions

A widespread organization of wilderness survivalists and protectors who maintain the balance between civilization and nature. Members include druids, rangers, and others who live close to the wild. The Enclave is not anti-civilization — they just believe the natural world must be respected, not merely exploited.

Local representative: [[reidoth]].

What They Want in Phandalin Region

  • Remove [[venomfang]] from [[ruins_of_thundertree]] before the dragon's presence unbalances the ecosystem.
  • Monitor threats to the wilderness — goblins, orcs, undead — that threaten natural balance.

Joining the Enclave

Reidoth can sponsor a party member after they help at Thundertree. Benefit: Enclave contacts provide wilderness survival aid (food, shelter, healing at Enclave-friendly sites). Enclave rangers can be called on as allies in wilderness encounters.
