The Zhentarim

FACTION · Major Factions

A mercenary and trade network of mercenaries, merchants, and assassins pursuing wealth and influence through any effective means. The Zhentarim is not evil by charter — it's opportunistic. Members are expected to be effective; ethics are a secondary concern. In practice this means the Zhentarim sits at the morally grey end of the faction spectrum.

Local representative: [[halia_thornton]].

What They Want in Phandalin

  • Take over the Redbrand's criminal operation — not destroy it, replace it with something more organized and profitable.
  • Gain a foothold in Phandalin's growing economy and eventually control the town's trade.

Joining the Zhentarim

Halia Thornton can sponsor a party member after the Redbrand quest. Benefit: access to black-market goods (magic items, rare materials, passage documents), Zhentarim safe houses, and Halia's personal contacts. Being Zhentarim means people with money trust you — and people who are scared of mercenaries leave you alone.
