The Harpers

FACTION · Major Factions

A secret society dedicated to preserving freedom, preventing the rise of tyranny, and protecting the "little folk" — ordinary people who lack power and resources. The Harpers operate through a network of agents placed throughout cities, towns, and trade routes. They gather information, identify threats, and act when conventional authority fails or won't.

Local representative: [[sister_garaele]].

What They Want in Phandalin

  • Recover [[bowgentles_spellbook|Bowgentle's spellbook]] from [[agatha|Agatha the Banshee]].
  • Keep an eye on the region's power balance — the Redbrands, the goblin threat, and the Black Spider all concern them.

Joining the Harpers

Sister Garaele can sponsor a party member. Benefit: access to Harper safehouses along major roads, healing resources, and an intelligence network. Harpers can request information from other agents. A Harper badge grants social trust in cities that respect the organization.
